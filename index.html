About

I am a photographer drawn to the quiet weight of light — the way it catches on old stone, dissolves into shadow, or holds a single unrepeatable moment.

My work lives at the intersection of documentary instinct and fine-art stillness. I do not chase the decisive moment so much as wait for it, trusting that patience and attention are the only real tools.

This series was made in Vence, a medieval hill town in the south of France, where the light falls like a conversation — oblique, unhurried, full of memory.
